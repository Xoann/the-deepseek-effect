Introduction

DeepSeek, a powerful yet cost-effective AI model, has significantly impacted the AI market, challenging Nvidia's dominance. This page outlines its impact and the key trends it has influenced.

Market Impact: Nvidia's Financial Hit

DeepSeek's introduction had a significant financial impact on Nvidia, the largest supplier of AI training hardware. With most AI models depending on Nvidia's GPUs, any major shift in the AI landscape has a direct effect on its market value. As DeepSeek gained attention, investors grew concerned about potential disruptions to Nvidia's dominance, leading to a sharp decline in its stock price. The drop reflected fears of reduced reliance on Nvidia’s hardware, impacting its valuation by billions. The following graph will illustrate this stock price drop over time.

Why Nvidia Was Hit the Hardest

One of the key reasons Nvidia experienced the largest financial hit is because its stock was overvalued due to its dominant role in the AI training space. DeepSeek’s disruptive emergence led investors to reassess Nvidia’s market positioning and future growth prospects.
