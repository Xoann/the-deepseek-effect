Introduction

DeepSeek, a powerful yet cost-effective AI model, has significantly impacted the AI market, challenging Nvidia's dominance. This page outlines its impact and the key trends it has influenced.

Market Impact: Nvidia's Financial Hit

DeepSeek's introduction had a significant financial impact on Nvidia, the largest supplier of AI training hardware. As DeepSeek gained attention, investors grew concerned about potential disruptions to Nvidia's dominance, leading to a sharp decline in its stock price.
